Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Returns & Refunds

At **Bitwise Cofe Shop**, we strive for customer satisfaction. If you're not happy with your order, please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refund

You can request a refund under the following conditions:

  • Incorrect order received
  • Damaged or spoiled product

Non-Refundable Items

We do not accept returns or refunds for:

  • Opened or consumed products
  • Customized drinks or special orders
  • Gift or promotional discounts

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please contact us within **7 days** of purchase with:

  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order number)
  • Photo evidence (if applicable)
  • Reason for the refund request

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within **5-7 business days**. The amount will be credited to the original payment method.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, reach out to us at **support@beanbliss.com** or visit our store.
